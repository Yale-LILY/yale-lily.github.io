Language, Information, and Learning at Yale (LILY)

This is the website for the LILY (Language, Information, and Learning at Yale) Lab at the Department of Computer Science at Yale University. The LILY Lab started in Spring 2017 with Professor Dragomir Radev joining Yale University. Our interests include:

  • Natural Language Processing
    • Information Retrieval
    • Text Summarization
    • Dialogue Systems
    • Question Answering
    • Humor Detection
  • Machine Learning
    • Neural Networks
    • Semi-supervised Learning
